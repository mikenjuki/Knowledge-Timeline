Monkey Pox Virus

Knowledge Timeline

  • 1958

    Monkeypox Virus first identified in monkeys in a Danish lab.

  • 1970

    First Monkeypox case in humans diagnosed in DRC.

  • 2000

    Enhanced disease surveillance system implemented in DRC.

  • 2003

    DRC sees an upward trend in Monkeypox cases.

  • 2008 - 2012

    Monkeypox reemerges in Nigeria.

  • 2017

    Monkeypox case identified in The United Kingdom.

  • 2019

    Monkeypox Virus first identified in monkeys in a Danish lab.

  • 2022

    Monkeypox spreads globally.
